About me.

I'm a writer and editor from London, based in Berlin since 2012. I write about culture – mostly art, though I also cover tech, music, film and whatever else I find interesting. I worked as Online and Associate Editor of Sleek from 2014-5. Now, I’m a freelance writer and editor with bylines at the Guardian, Frieze, Artforum, The Economist, Dazed, and many more.
